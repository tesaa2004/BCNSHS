Strands We Offer in Senior High School

BCNSHS offers three academic tracks for senior high school: STEM, ABM, HUMSS;
and three technical-vocational tracks: ICT, AUTO, HE.

Go Back to Home

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics
(STEM)

The STEM strand emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving, and hands-on experimentation to foster innovation and prepare students for higher education or careers in STEM-related fields such as engineering, medicine, computer science, research, and more.

Click To Know More

Accountancy, Business and Management
(ABM)

The ABM Strand is designed for individuals who intend to pursue business-related courses in higher education or embark on careers in business, entrepreneurship, and other related fields.

Click To Know More

Humanities and Social Science
(HUMSS)

The HUMSS Strand caters to those who are contemplating pursuing college courses in journalism, communication arts, liberal arts, education, and other social science-related fields. This particular strand focuses on honing and enhancing communication skills.

Click To Know More

Information, Communication and Technology
(ICT)

ICT Strand focuses on equipping students with knowledge and skills in various aspects of technology, computer science, programming, and communication. It prepares students for careers in fields such as software development, network administration, database management, and digital media.

Click To Know More

Home Economics
(HE)

HE is an educational strand that provides students with knowledge and skills related to domestic and practical aspects of life. It encompasses various subjects such as cooking, sewing, interior design, child development, family and consumer sciences, and personal finance.

Click To Know More

Automotive
(AUTO)

The AUTO Strand focuses on providing students with knowledge and skills related to automotive technology and mechanics. It is designed to prepare students for careers in the automotive industry, such as automotive technicians, mechanics, service advisors, or automotive engineers.

LEARN ADVANCED SUBJECTS

BCNSHS does not only offer core subjects but also specialized subjects wherein the curriculum in MATHEMATICS and SCIENCE are more advanced with more in-depth learning about the different topics.

BE A YOUNG RESEARCHER

BCNSHS starts the students young by adding RESEARCH as one of the studentsâ€™ subjects starting Grade 7. This allows four years of intricate learning about the proper procedures before and during an experimentation; and how to properly write a research paper. This also hones the skills of the students when it comes to their problem solving and critical thinking.

SHOWCASE WHAT YOU CAN DO

BCNSHS gives students, whatever their age and grade level is, a chance to showcase their skills and abilities through various competitions both curated by the school and events outside the school.

ENHANCE YOUR CREATIVITY

Being a Science High student does not mean you should limit yourself in only doing academic activities. Therefore, BCNSHS encourages and helps each student to find their own creativity through the MAPEH subject and the different Clubs that they offer.

BECOME SOCIALLY AWARE

BCNSHS encourages their students to be aware of the current news and events on both local, national, and global settings. As a student of Science and Research, it is encouraged that we are aware of what is happening and become part of the solution.
